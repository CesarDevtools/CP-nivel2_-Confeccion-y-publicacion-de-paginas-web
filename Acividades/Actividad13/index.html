Gymshark is a fitness apparel brand that has gained immense popularity in recent years. Founded in 2012 by Ben Francis and a group of friends, Gymshark started as a small screen-printing operation in a garage. The brand quickly grew into a global phenomenon, known for its stylish and functional workout clothing.
